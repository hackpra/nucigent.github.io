About us

who we are?

NUCIGENT provides one stop automated solution for your trade and industry.
Depending upon the size and field of your organization, we have different products and
services to meet your requirements. We provide the optimum and customized solutions
made for your organization. NUCIGENT began its business operation as a hardware
and network solutions providing company in May 2019.
We know digital has completely changed how brands compete. To differentiate and win
more business, having a digital-first mindset is key even for physical locations—it’s the
way to meet consumers in every moment and transform transactions into meaningful
interactions.
Digital first but not digital only, we’re positioned to help our clients bridge their digital
and physical operations—helping them connect with consumers anytime, anywhere and
streamline their business so they can differentiate, compete and win in our evolving
digital world.
